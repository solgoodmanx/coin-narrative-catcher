# Signal Framework (Narrative-First)

## 1) Catalyst Strength (0-5)

- 5: Direct trigger from high-cred actor/protocol/team account, clear event
- 3: Trigger from credible niche account, medium reach
- 1: No clear catalyst, only random mentions

## 2) Credibility Graph (0-5)

Score the *originating people*:
- Proven operator credentials
- Relevant previous builds/ships
- Known ecosystem trust links

Low score if identity is vague, recycled, or unverifiable.

## 3) Narrative Quality (0-5)

- Is it a coherent thesis or just meme slogans?
- Does market have a simple repeatable story to spread?
- Is there a believable path to next attention wave?

## 4) Social Propagation Quality (0-5)

- High: independent accounts add original reasoning
- Mid: mixed original + repeated reposts
- Low: mostly copy-paste shill spam

## 5) Tape Confirmation (0-5) — secondary

Use only as confirm/deny layer:
- Rising attention + improving liquidity + clean participation = confirm
- Narrative hype with weak liquidity/rapid fade = caution

---

## Red Flags (hard cautions)

- Narrative depends on unverifiable claims only
- Catalyst is old but being recycled as “new”
- Few wallets can move the whole chart instantly
- Social proof is circular (same accounts quoting each other)
- No direct connection between claimed builder and token artifact

---

## Scoring Output

- 20-25: Strong narrative setup
- 14-19: Tradeable but fragile
- 8-13: Mostly noise/spec churn
- <8: Low-quality setup

Always add 2-3 invalidation triggers.
